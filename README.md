A curated list of AI tools and platforms that let you turn images into videos for free or with a generous free tier.  
Perfect for creators, marketers, and developers testing image-to-video workflows.

> Note  
> Free here usually means free tier, free trial, or limited monthly credits, not infinite free usage.

## What this list is

This repository tracks online tools and platforms that support:

- Image to video  
- Sometimes also text to video and video to video  
- With a clearly usable free tier or trial

The goal is to give creators and developers a single place to discover and compare tools.

## Evaluation criteria

A tool is included if:

- It supports image-to-video in a browser or via a simple UI  
- It has a free tier, free trial, or free credits that a new user can realistically use  
- The service is open to the public and not only private beta  

Nice-to-have points:

- Clear pricing page  
- Public examples or demo gallery  
- API or SDK for developers
